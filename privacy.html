Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 5 June 2026

1. Introduction

CivicPath Works (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected in accordance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

2. Data We Collect

By using the website and submission form, you may provide:

  • Name and contact information, including mobile or WhatsApp number.
  • Professional details, such as area of expertise and work experience.
  • Residency status, including Singapore Citizen or Permanent Resident status.

3. Purpose of Collection

This data is collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To evaluate suitability for managed digital opportunities.
  • To communicate via WhatsApp or phone regarding opportunity options.
  • To provide professional career consultancy and support.

4. Disclosure of Data

Your privacy is a priority. Personal data is not sold or rented to third parties. A profile is only shared with opportunity partners after a personal consultation and verbal or written consent.

5. Data Security

Appropriate administrative and technical measures are implemented to keep data confidential and secure at the CBD-based office.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to or correction of your personal data, or withdraw consent at any time by contacting the team at hello@civicpathworks.sg.
