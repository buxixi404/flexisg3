Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) Notice

At CivicPath Works, privacy is taken seriously. In compliance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA), this notice outlines how personal data is managed.

1. Collection of Personal Data

Name, WhatsApp number, expertise, and residency status are collected specifically to assess eligibility for managed digital opportunities within the network.

2. Purpose of Use

Data is used solely for:

  • Assessing Match Potential for active or upcoming opportunities.
  • Contacting applicants via WhatsApp or phone for professional consultations.
  • Managing administrative requirements of an opportunity engagement, should an applicant join the team.

3. Consent & Disclosure

By submitting the form, you consent to being contacted for the purposes stated above. Personal data is never shared with third-party clients or partners without first having a personal conversation and obtaining verbal or written consent.

4. Data Security & Retention

Data is stored securely and is only accessible by authorized consultants at the CBD office. Information is retained only as long as necessary to provide opportunity options.

5. Access & Withdrawal

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data at any time. For questions regarding PDPA practices, contact the Data Protection representative at hello@civicpathworks.sg.
